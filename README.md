# tinkertip
Tinkerable Tip Calculator

Nowadays when you go to buy anything, even a cup of coffee, they put up a display that makes it easy to add automated tip. However, I'd rather understand the tip and tax amount visually on a pie chart. This app allows you to enter the total amount at the top. You can enter the tax percentage or tax amount then the tip percentage or amount.  The bottom will show you a visual pie chart of what portion you're paying is tax, tip, or cost of the items.  
If you're like me and want to tweak the tip to numbers you like, but also keep within acceptable tip range, you can move the sliders for the tip around. You can also grab the green pie wedge to change the tip amount/percentage. The pie chart will update accordingly.
